About me

Hi, my name is Danny Yang and I am studying computer science at Cornell University.


Here at Cornell I am a member of Cornell Cup, a robotics project team, and I work as a Django developer at StartupTree, a startup based in the eHub at Cornell.


In between schoolwork and side projects, I enjoy going to farmer's markets, renaissance fairs, museums, and Hackathons. I enjoy learning about design and history, and keeping up with current events.


I'm currently learning about data science using R, data visualization using d3.js, and Django web development. In the future I plan to learn more about statistics, data science, and machine learning, while maintaining a solid foundation in core CS skills.
